1.使用内置的Camera相机

```java
//take picture
public void capture(){
    Intent cameraIntent = new Intent(MediaStore.ACTION_IMAGE_CAPTURE);
    //cameraIntent.putExtra(MediaStore.EXTRA_OUTPUT,getSavePath());
    cameraIntent.putExtra(MediaStore.EXTRA_OUTPUT,insertUri());
    startActivityForResult(cameraIntent,CAPTURE_RESULT);
}
```
